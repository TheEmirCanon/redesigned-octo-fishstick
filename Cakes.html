How far in advance should I order?

For standard cakes, 3 days is enough. For custom designs, order 1 week ahead. Wedding cakes need 2 weeks minimum.

Do you do cake tastings?

Yes! For wedding cakes and large orders, we offer free tastings. Book an appointment.

Can you match a specific color?

Absolutely! Send us a color code or photo and we'll match it perfectly.

Do you deliver outside Lagos?

Yes, we can arrange transport to other states. Contact us for a quote.

How should I store the cake?

Keep in a cool place away from sunlight. If not eating same day, refrigerate and bring to room temperature before serving.

Can you make eggless cakes?

Yes! We have eggless options available. Just let us know when ordering.

Do you have sugar-free options?

Yes, we offer diabetic-friendly cakes with alternative sweeteners.
